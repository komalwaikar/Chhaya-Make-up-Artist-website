Chhaya Make-up Artist

Many years before her parent’s could give her permission, chhaya would apply the hottest and the coolest shades of lip color to her lips and nail paint to her tiny nails.” I would sneak in to my much older cousins’ house next door and go bananas on the lipsticks and compact powders! “.Soon chhaya’s exploits with makeup were in high demand from her classmates and friends. She set up a small dressing table and a mirror as her first studio where she would transform her friends and relatives! This passion of making everyone more beautiful led her into the world of professional makeup artistry! Coming straight to the present, chhaya (a lawyer by education) stopped practicing about 10 years ago and instead decided to pursue her interest and passion. After freelancing for a period, she opened her studio in Akurdi .She now runs a full fledged Salon and Academy as well. chhaya has received professional training from the likes of Hollywood makeup artists and other well known veterans. Parul has also received training in Airbrush makeup by world renowned TEMPTU and is certified by them. She is a specialist in Airbrush Makeup and accomplishes more than a whopping 500 Airbrush makeups every year!

Although chhaya excels in all kinds of looks and makeup her primary passion remains Bridal and Occasion makeup. Her brides display a class of their own and differ from run of the mill ones. She believes in bringing out the inherent beauty in you rather than create a fake plastic look. Although chhaya has come a long way from initial passion, the original driving force that led her into the beauty industry still remains as it was. Parul still finds the most satisfaction when a client feels beautiful. “After I finish, I love the look on their face when they look in the mirror and see the results. No prizes for guessing that they actually feel beautiful. ”
